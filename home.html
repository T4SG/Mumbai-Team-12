Who We Are?

Happy Hearts Fund is active in 9 countries and has rebuilt 116 schools that were damaged or destroyed by natural disasters!

What We Do?

We work during the gap period when children are forgotten after emergency response is complete, bringing hope and empowerment to generations of children and entire communities.

Why We Do It?

we care about helping children around the world.

Since When?

Happy Hearts Fund, founded in 2006 by Petra Nemcova after she survived the 2004 Indian Ocean Tsunami, rebuilds safe-resilient schools in areas impacted by natural disasters.
